# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-01-08

## Who we are
This app (“CRITICAL”) is provided by [SmallTaskForce]. Contact: admin@smalltaskforce.com.

## Data we collect and process
- **Notifications:** Accessed to detect alerts from selected apps. Processed locally; not sent to our servers.
- **Local storage:** App settings (selected apps, volume, duration, custom audio paths) stored on-device.
- **Network access:** Used for standard app functionality; no personal data is sent to our servers.
- **Audio:** Plays alarm sounds/ringtones per your configuration.

## How we use data
- Trigger alarms based on notifications from apps you select.
- Apply your saved preferences (duration, volume, audio file, monitored packages).

## Sharing of data
- We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
- Data remains on your device unless you explicitly share it.

## Children’s privacy
Not directed to children under 13. If a child’s data is suspected, contact us to address it.

## Security
Data is kept locally using platform storage and permissions. No server-side storage is used.

## Your choices
- Disable notification access in system settings.
- Uninstall the app to remove locally stored data.

## Permissions explained
- **Notification Listener:** Reads notifications from apps you choose.
- **Post Notifications:** Shows local alerts.
- **Wake Lock / Receive Boot Completed / Vibrate / Modify Audio Settings:** Ensures alarms run reliably and can play audio.
- **Internet / Network State:** Standard connectivity; no personal data is sent to our servers.
